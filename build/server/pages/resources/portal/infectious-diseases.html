Infectious Diseases (Non HIV/TB)

Introduction: We tend to focus disproportionally on HIV & TB, but there are other common and/ or dangerous infections that should not be forgotten. As most of these have public health implications, there are excellent NDOH or NICD guidelines that cover them.
